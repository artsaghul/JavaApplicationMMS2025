вЂ‹Information Technology is a broad field involving the use of computers, storage, networking, and other physical devices to create, process, store, secure, and exchange all forms of electronic data. Today, IT is the foundation of global commerce, healthcare, and governance.

вЂ‹Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. These "cyberattacks" are usually aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information; extorting money from users; or interrupting normal business processes.
вЂ‹Key Areas of Cybersecurity:
вЂ‹Network Security: Securing a computer network from intruders, whether targeted attackers or opportunistic malware.
вЂ‹Application Security: Keeping software and devices free of threats by ensuring security is built-in during the design phase.
вЂ‹Cloud Security: Protecting data and infrastructure hosted in environments like AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud.
вЂ‹Incident Response: The organized approach to managing and addressing the aftermath of a security breach or attack.
вЂ‹Section 3: Reasons for Specializing in Cybersecurity
вЂ‹Choosing Cybersecurity as a career path is a strategic decision based on the following factors:
вЂ‹Critical Global Demand: As Nigeria and the rest of the world undergo rapid digital transformation, the threat landscape expands. From banking apps to government databases, the need for "digital police" is at an all-time high.
вЂ‹High Career Resilience: While some IT roles can be automated by AI, cybersecurity requires human intuition, ethical judgment, and complex problem-solving that remains difficult for machines to replicate perfectly.
вЂ‹Dynamic Nature: It is a field that never stays still. Hackers develop new methods every day (such as sophisticated phishing or ransomware), which means a specialist must constantly learn and evolve, preventing professional stagnation.
вЂ‹Financial Reward: Due to the high stakes involved in data protection and the shortage of skilled professionals globally, cybersecurity roles typically offer some of the highest salaries in the IT sector.
